SITE ARCHITECTURE NOTES:
=========================
- Single-page landing at /en (English) and / (Romanian)
- Sections (Team, Prices, Gallery, Gift Cards, Contact) are anchor links on landing page
- Individual service category pages at /en/services/{category-slug}
- Services index at /en/services
- Booking flow is a multi-step modal overlay (5 steps with progress dots)
- Admin/Dashboard pages at /admin (require authentication)
- WhatsApp integration for customer messaging
- Google Maps embed for location
- Bilingual: Romanian (default) + English
